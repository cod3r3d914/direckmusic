Bio

Direck is a Raleigh, NC artist creating music rooted in real life, emotion, and transformation. What started as poetry written to process anxiety, pain, and chaos evolved into music driven by storytelling, ambition, reflection, and raw energy.

Inspired by rhythm from an early age, Direck blends emotion and perspective into records that move between survival, motivation, adrenaline, and honesty — creating music for every state of mind.

Music meant to be felt, not just heard.
